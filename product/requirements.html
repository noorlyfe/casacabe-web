System requirements

Casacabe runs locally on your computer. You need a machine that can run a modern desktop app. Internet is only required for purchase and optional email support — the app works fully offline after install.

Product

Try it before you buy App tour Overview System requirements

macOS

  • macOS 11 Big Sur or later (64-bit Intel or Apple Silicon)
  • About 200 MB free disk space for the app and your data
  • Works offline after install
  • Recommended: 8 GB RAM or more for smooth performance with large datasets

Windows

  • Windows 10 or later (64-bit)
  • About 200 MB free disk space for the app and your data
  • Works offline after install
  • Recommended: 8 GB RAM or more for smooth performance with large datasets

One license, both platforms

Your purchase includes installers for both Mac and Windows. Install on whichever computer you use for admin work — office PC, MacBook at home, or both.

Not supported in v1

  • iPhone, iPad, or Android — desktop only
  • Web browser version — install required
  • Linux — not available in v1
  • Windows 32-bit or Windows 7/8
  • macOS 10.15 Catalina or earlier

Internet connection

Casacabe does not require an internet connection for daily use. You only need internet for:

  • Initial purchase and license download
  • Emailing support@casacabe.com
  • Future v1 update downloads (optional — you choose when to update)

Your data stays local

Restaurant records are stored in a local database on the machine where you install Casacabe. Back up your computer or export reports regularly. We do not host your data in the cloud. Read more in our privacy policy and FAQ.
