Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

Casacabe collects only your email address and name at the time of purchase, processed by Stripe. We do not collect any data from inside the app.

2. Your Restaurant Data

All data you enter into Casacabe — revenue, expenses, staff, invoices — is stored locally on your computer only. We have no access to it. We never see it. We never store it.

3. License Activation

Your license key is validated locally on your computer. No data is sent to our servers during activation or at any other time.

4. Stripe

Payments are processed by Stripe. We receive confirmation that a payment was made and your email address. We do not store your card details. See Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Cookies

Our website (casacabe.com) does not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify you personally.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@casacabe.com
