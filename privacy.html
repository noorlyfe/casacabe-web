Privacy

Last updated: June 2026. Plain language summary: your restaurant numbers stay on your device.

Your data stays local

Casacabe stores your restaurant information in a database on your Mac or Windows computer. We do not operate a cloud service that holds your sales, expenses, invoices, or staff records.

What we do not collect from the app

Because the app runs locally, we do not receive your daily revenue, labor costs, vendor names, or any other backoffice data by default. We are not building a data business on your restaurant.

What we may receive

  • Purchase: When you buy, payment is processed by Stripe. We receive what Stripe shares for the transaction (email, payment status). We do not receive your card number.
  • Email: If you contact support@casacabe.com, we receive what you send us.
  • License: We may store your email and license key to validate your purchase and send download links.

Backups and exports

You can export reports from Casacabe. You are responsible for backing up your computer and any files you export. We cannot recover lost local data for you.

Children

Casacabe is business software. It is not directed at children under 13.

Questions

Email support@casacabe.com for privacy questions.
